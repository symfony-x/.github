# Symfony-X Organization Charter

## Mission

To build a deterministic, composable Symfony architecture.

---

## Structure

- Foundation
- Identity
- Capabilities
- Compositions
- Governance

---

## Principles

- Identity must be explicit
- Capabilities must be isolated
- Compositions assemble applications
- Governance enforces correctness

---

## Rules

- No implicit architecture
- No hidden dependencies
- No cross-layer violations
